Our Story

Crafting Extraordinary Vegetarian Memories in Siliguri

Welcome to The Basiil, where culinary passion meets the pristine standards of 100% pure vegetarian dining. Nestled elegantly on the 1st Floor of City Centre Mall (E Block, right above Domino's), we have established ourselves as a premier sanctuary for food connoisseurs, families, and shoppers looking for an unparalleled gastronomic experience in Siliguri.

Our philosophy is simple: vegetarian food should never be a compromise. It should be a celebration of rich textures, vibrant colors, and deep, authentic flavors. Every single dish that leaves our state-of-the-art, strictly vegetarian kitchen is crafted from scratch using handpicked, locally sourced vegetables, premium dairy, and an exquisite blend of traditional and contemporary spices.

A Symphony of Three Cuisines

At The Basiil, we understand that a great dining experience requires variety. That is why we have carefully curated a multi-cuisine menu handled by specialized chefs:

  • The North Indian Heritage: From the slow-cooked, buttery richness of our Dal Makhani to the velvety, aromatic bliss of Paneer Butter Masala, we bring the true soul of Punjab and Old Delhi to your plate.
  • The Pan-Asian Wok Magic: Sizzling high-flame creations, crisp colorful vegetables, and perfectly balanced sauces define our Indo-Chinese menu, featuring crowd favorites like Veg Hakka Noodles and Chilli Paneer.
  • Modern Fast Food Comforts: Perfect for a quick hangout or a mall break, our crispy burgers, artisanal sandwiches, and perfectly seasoned chatkara fries offer premium comfort food at its finest.
"We don't just serve meals; we curate dining experiences that bring families together and turn first-time visitors into lifelong patrons."
100% Pure Veg Sanctuary: Complete separation of utensils and ingredients for absolute peace of mind.
Gourmet Quality Standards: We use only premium-grade cottage cheese, select oils, and fresh produce.
Premium Mall Ambience: Fully air-conditioned, elegant seating arrangement perfect for family gatherings.
Strict Hygiene Protocols: Zero-compromise cleanliness from food preparation to contactless presentation.
